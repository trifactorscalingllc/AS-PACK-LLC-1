The Collection

Our Roasting Partners

We represent a select group of master roasters from Italy's most celebrated coffee regions. Each brings a unique profile, from the dark, robust blends of Napoli to the lighter, aromatic roasts of the North.

Napoli, Campania

Torrefazione Partenope

A family-owned roaster specializing in the classic Neapolitan style. Dark, intense, and full-bodied, their espresso blends cut perfectly through milk and offer a rich, lingering crema.

  • Specialty: Traditional Espresso Blends
  • Ideal for: High-volume cafes, traditional Italian dining
Milano, Lombardia

Caffè Nordico

Bringing a modern, refined approach to traditional Northern Italian roasting. Known for their 100% Arabica blends that are sweeter, fruitier, and incredibly balanced.

  • Specialty: Delicate Arabica Blends, Single Origins
  • Ideal for: Fine dining, specialty coffee shops
Firenze, Toscana

Artigiano Toscano

A boutique roaster using slow, wood-fired roasting techniques developed in the 1920s. Their beans yield an espresso an incredibly smooth, chocolatey profile with virtually zero acidity.

  • Specialty: Wood-fired Roasting
  • Ideal for: Boutique hotels, artisanal bakeries
